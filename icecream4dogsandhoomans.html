How To Make Ice Cream For Dogs And Hoomans

⚠️CONTAINS EGGS AND PEANUT BUTTER AND MAYBE SOME OTHER ALLERGIES SO CHECK INGREDIENTS FIRST!⚠️

Ingredients

    Ice Cream

  • 3 bananas
  • 1/2 tbsp Honey
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of low-fat coconut milk
  • 1.5 TBSP of unsweetened peanut butter
  • Ice cream cones!!! You can't have ice cream without cones!!

STUFF TO USE

  • Stove (possibly?)
  • Blender
  • An ice cream scooper (or spoon)
  • Pan with bigger sides than a normal pan for pancakes

EXTRA

  • An adult to help you

Method

  1. 1. Slice the bananas and freeze overnight.
  2. 2. Put the pan on the stove and set it to gentle heat then pour the coconut milk into the pan and break the two eggs into the pan.
  3. 3. Whisk continuously for 7 minutes or until the custard thickens and coats the back of a spoon.
  4. 4. Grab your blender and pour in the custard, frozen bananas, 1/2 TBSP of honey and 1.5 TBSP of peanut butter.
  5. 5. Blend until smooth.
  6. 6. Freeze until firm.
  7. 7. Scoop ice cream into cone.
  8. 8.
