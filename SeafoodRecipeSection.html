How To Make Seafood Ramen

Ingredients

    ----SEAFOOD----

  • 2 filled skinned Cod or any white fish of your choice
  • 50 g Squid rings
  • 75 g raw king Prawn
  • 50 g Fishsticks
  • 1 tbsp Chilli garlic oi, keep it to garlic oil if serving to kids
  • 1 tbsp of Oil to fry
